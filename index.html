#T-Shirts Gives Lifes

Charity fund for assistance to sick,low-income children and orphans.

The Foundation's mission is to help children in hospitals and orphangaes find hope by bringing together professional athletes-people who have achieved great heights through faith in dreams and strength of character. The foundation gives childern opportunity to communicate with athletes inside a hospital or orphangae, and also organaizes visits to Centeral sports events for them.
